Gold Tier Member

  • Prominent Member Directory Listing: Gain maximum visibility with a premium listing in our online member directory, including enhanced profile options and increased searchability.
  • Lead Generation Opportunities: Participate in exclusive programs designed to connect you with potential customers and generate qualified leads.
  • Member-to-Member Discounts: Enjoy significant savings on products and services offered by fellow chamber members.
  • Priority Access to Events & Programs: Be the first to register for highly sought-after events, workshops, and networking opportunities.
  • Ambassador Program Invitation: Become an ambassador for the chamber and gain valuable leadership experience while promoting your business.

Silver Tier Member

  • Standard Member Directory Listing: Get listed in our comprehensive online member directory, searchable by potential customers.
  • Discounted Event Registration: Receive a discount on registration fees for chamber events, workshops, and networking opportunities.
  • Member-to-Member Referral Network: Tap into a network of trusted businesses through member referrals and recommendations.
  • Monthly E-Newsletter: Stay informed about upcoming events, industry trends, and advocacy efforts with our exclusive member newsletter.
  • Legislative Advocacy Updates: Gain insights on local and regional legislation impacting your business through chamber updates.

Bronze Tier Member

  • Basic Member Directory Listing: Gain a basic listing in our online member directory, connecting you with potential customers.
  • Member Discounts: Enjoy exclusive discounts on chamber-sponsored events and programs.
  • Access to Member Resources: Utilize valuable online resources and tools to support your business growth.
  • Networking Opportunities: Attend select chamber events and connect with other businesses in the community.
  • Ribbon Cutting Ceremony: Celebrate your grand opening or relocation with a ribbon-cutting ceremony facilitated by the chamber (upon request).

Non-Profit Partner

  • Joint Advocacy Efforts: Collaborate with the chamber on advocacy initiatives that benefit both businesses and non-profit organizations.
  • Promotional Opportunities: Leverage the chamber's communication channels to raise awareness for your non-profit's mission and programs.
  • Volunteer Recruitment Support: Gain access to the chamber's network to connect with potential volunteers who can support your organization.
  • Discounted Chamber Events: Receive discounted registration fees for relevant chamber events to showcase your non-profit's work.
  • Grant Opportunities: Be eligible to apply for grants offered by the chamber or its partners to support your non-profit's goals.
